Version 8.01 (2004-01-30)

This is the first release of the 8.0x series under the GPL.

The most significant new feature since 7.0x is support for DeviceN colorspaces, up to 8 colorants total at 8 bits per component. Also, pdfwrite has seen considerable improvement in font handling, and many other bug fixes.

Incompatible changes

No incompatible changes are expected in this release.
